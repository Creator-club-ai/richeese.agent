# ResearchOutput

Purpose:

- turn one chosen source or selected signal into usable evidence

Required fields:

- `Topic`
- `Research Depth`
- `Source Inventory`
- `What Happened`
- `Usable Points`
- `Direction Cues`
- `Risks or Gaps`
- `Source Strength`
- `Fact Risk`
- `Recommendation`

Optional fields when useful:

- `Key Quotes`
- `Recurring Themes`
- `Wiki Targets`

Recommendation values:

- `send to content-os-planner`
- `stop`
